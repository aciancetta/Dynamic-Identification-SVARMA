Dynamic Identification in VARMA Models

Dynamic Identification in VARMA Models

Alessandro Ciancetta

2021-07-13

Abstract

This book present the general framework of VARMA models and introduces the notion of non-fundamentalness. The problem is assessed using the methodology of Gourieroux, Manfort and Renne (2019), which is illustrated in detail, within the framework of non-Gaussian and independent structural shocks of the corresponding structural model. Simulations and application to real-world data are presented.
